Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is it legal to use these YouTube tools?

You should only download videos for which you have the copyright owner's permission. It is against YouTube's terms of service to download content without permission. Our tools are intended for use with your own content, public domain content, or content for which you have explicit rights.

Are the tools completely free to use?

Yes, all tools listed on this page are 100% free. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations on usage.

Do I need to install any software?

No, all our tools are web-based. You do not need to download or install any software or browser extensions. Everything works directly in your web browser on any device.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy. We do not store your videos, audio, or any uploaded data on our servers. All processing is done in real-time, and your data is automatically deleted after a short period. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
